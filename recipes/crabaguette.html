Pepsi boiled Crabaguette

Description

A lovely 2-hour slow boiled crab in a crusty french baguette

Ingredients

  • 1 Crab
  • 2 litres of Pepsi
  • 1 French Baguette

Steps

  1. Bring 2 litres of Pepsi to a soft boil
  2. gently place live crab into Pepsi and boil for 2 hours
  3. Meanwhile, cut baguette in half and toast
  4. Once crab is cooked through, place inside baguette and pair with your favourite riesling
  5. Enjoy!
